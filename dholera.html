Project Overview

Eleque Infra: Your Gateway to Dholera’s Future

Dholera is India’s first and most ambitious smart city project, poised to become a global economic hub under the Delhi-Mumbai Industrial Corridor (DMIC). Spanning 920 sq km—twice the size of Delhi—Dholera features world-class infrastructure, digital connectivity, and an upcoming international airport. It also leads in sustainability, with an 11% green area and expanding solar power capabilities. Eleque Infra empowers forward-thinking investors to secure land parcels starting at just ₹10 lakh, ensuring a transparent and future-proof opportunity for long-term growth. Join us as we shape tomorrow’s urban landscape, one plot at a time.

Fill out the form below to receive your free brochure and consultation. Our team will guide you every step of the way—your future in India’s most transformative city awaits.
